We Are Hiring

We are currently seeking an Accountant for our office in Cupertino, California. The ideal candidate is a team-player, dedicated to excellence, hardworking, and committed to our client's success.

Job Description: Mainly concerned with research and application of principles in accounting, economic theory, financial and taxation fields, and formulating plans to aid in advising clients on the recognition, interpretation, and resolution of financial problems through training in accounting, business management, and computer and communication skills.

Responsibilities include:

  1. Examine, compile and analyze accounting records of clients to reach analysis results for the use of giving business advice and preparing financial statements, tax returns or business plans;
  2. Prepare and interpret financial reports and statistical charts and tables detailing findings;
  3. Assist in formulating recommendations or plans to interpret and resolve clients' problems; and
  4. Participate in research projects concerning business and tax planning, tax compliance and saving strategies.

Master's degree in Accounting from an accredited university and 3 months experience in the job offered or related field required.

Ability to manage multiple tasks; willingness to learn and motivated to succeed; and good communication skills.
