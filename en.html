We are AGAPE CPA FIRM

Agape CPA Firm was founded by Yi Lin, CPA in Cupertino, California in 2009. The mission of our firm is to provide high-quality accounting and tax services, with the goal of using our profession to serve our community. In the beginning of 2013, we merged with a reputable local CPA firm, Davis Chang, CPA, and formed Chang and Lin Accountancy, Corp. During this period, we specialized in foreign assets reporting and represented taxpayers in front of the Service, helping many taxpayers streamline their tax filing. In the summer of 2019, we changed our name back to Agape CPA Firm. We hope that continue to carry the passion that started in 2009.

Today, our tax and accounting professionals look forward to contributing our knowledge and experience to our valuable clients. On this website, you can find articles written by Davis Chang, CPA and Yi Lin, CPA–as well as radio and TV interviews from the past years. We hope that these resources can help you to understand more complicated tax-related topics. You can also scan the bar code below to join our Webchat or Line group.
